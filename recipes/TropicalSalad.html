They hydrate and refresh, since vegetables are more than 90% water.

They provide nutrients such as vitamins, minerals and heart-healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 270 gr vegetable spirals
  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 carrot
  • C/s butter
  • 2 Greek yogurt
  • C/s pomegranate sauce

Recipe Instructions

  1. First we will put water in a saucepan to boil, and when it is boiling add the macaroni.
  2. While the macaroni is done, we will cut the carrot, the banana and the apple (it is advisable to leave the banana in the refrigerator and the apple in a glass of water so that they do not oxidize).
  3. When we already have it we will make the sauce mixing the yogurt with the sauce.
  4. Next we will add butter in a frying pan and when it is hot we can over medium heat the banana, carrot and apple.
  5. And to finish, we will strain the macaroni and we will mix it with the sauce and the sofrito.
  6. (Preferably consume when cold)
