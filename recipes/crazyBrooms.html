Crazy Brooms

this ideal plan for Halloween, quite simple that will make you stand out.

don't miss out on this super cool recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of bread sticks
  • 1 package cooked ham
  • 1 jar of black olives

Recipe Instructions

  1. Cut the cooked ham into strips and roll them in the bread sticks.
  2. Cut the black olives for the spiders.
